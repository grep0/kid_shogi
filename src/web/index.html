Rules
# Sente Gote

Rules

Pieces

Lion Any direction, 1 step
Giraffe Orthogonal, 1 step
Elephant Diagonal, 1 step
Chick Forward, 1 step
Hen ★ Orthogonal + forward diagonals

★ Chick promotes to Hen upon reaching the far rank

Captures & Drops

  • Captured pieces go to your hand
  • On your turn you may drop a piece from hand onto any empty square instead of moving
  • Chicks may not be dropped on the last rank
  • Dropped Hens revert to Chicks

Winning

  • Capture the opponent's Lion, or
  • Advance your Lion to the far rank and it is not captured on the next move
